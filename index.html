    Like many women around the world, I resonate with the joy, emotion, and creativity that fashion brings. My journey took a dramatic turn when I moved from India to London as a first-generation immigrant. Life changed 360 degrees, and with it came the challenge of proving to myself—and to the world—that it’s possible to break barriers, embrace a new culture, and build something extraordinary from scratch.


    Coming from a humble and honest family, where my father served in the Government of India from the age of 18 until his retirement, I knew I wanted to create a different path—one that celebrated courage, individuality, and ambition. As an immigrant navigating different languages, cultures, and mindsets, I learned that it’s not just okay to dream differently—it’s powerful.


    Completing my Fashion Styling course at FIT was a turning point. It gave me clarity, confidence, and the vision to step into the world I always imagined—and more, faster than I could have ever expected.


    Today, I start this new chapter, from zero, with a venture that is as much yours as it is mine. I invite you to join me from day one and experience a glimpse of my world.
